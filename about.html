Our Story

Change starts in villages, not boardrooms.

Here's what led us here, and where we're going.

A pattern we couldn't ignore

Rural India's biggest challenges rarely show up one at a time. A lack of schooling feeds into limited livelihoods. Limited livelihoods strain the environment. Environmental strain makes everything else harder.

Healthy Earth Foundation began with people who spent real time in these communities and noticed the same thing again and again: help that focused on a single issue tended to fade once attention moved elsewhere. We wanted something that could last, so we combined good science, practical technology, and villagers themselves as genuine partners rather than recipients.

Mission, Vision, Values

Mission

What We Do

Education, women's livelihoods, environmental restoration, health, and water security, shaped around what each village actually asks for, not a fixed plan we hand over.

Vision

Where We're Headed

A model built in Western Uttar Pradesh that genuinely travels from one village to the next, not a single success story that stays put.

Values

How We Work

Fewer commitments that hold up for years, rather than many that only look good in a photo.

Our Founders

Healthy Earth Foundation was founded by researchers and professionals, including PhD scholars and postgraduates from respected Indian institutions, with backgrounds spanning environmental research, infrastructure, community welfare, and sustainable development. Rigorous science paired with genuine compassion, applied locally and consistently, is what produces change that actually lasts.

Where We Work

Our work is currently underway in villages across Western Uttar Pradesh, the proving ground for an approach we hope to carry much further into rural India as we grow.
