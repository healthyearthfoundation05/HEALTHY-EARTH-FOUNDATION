Our Impact

Real work, told honestly.

As our programs grow, this is where we'll keep an honest record of what we've actually done together with the villages we work in.

Community Initiatives & Milestones

Every entry here reflects something we did with a community, not just for one. That distinction matters to us.

Plantation Drive: Hasanpur Kalan Village

We brought together residents of Hasanpur Kalan for a community plantation drive aimed at restoring green space and building lasting awareness around trees, biodiversity, and sustainable living. The trees are still there. So is the conversation we started.

Cleanliness & Hygiene Awareness Program

We ran a campaign focused on better sanitation habits, personal hygiene, and waste management, with the goal of sparking action the community could keep going on its own long after we left.

Skill Development & Women's Empowerment Initiatives

We're helping build real opportunity in these villages through skill development, with particular focus on women's independence and livelihoods that don't depend on outside support.

Clean Energy & Pollution Awareness Campaigns

We're pushing for cleaner fuels and speaking plainly about the harm of burning plastics, waste, and wood in the open. That means working directly with communities, and with local industries and stakeholders too, to protect the air people actually breathe.
